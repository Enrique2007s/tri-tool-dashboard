Tri-Tool Dashboard

Tri-Tool Dashboard, used for monitoring and managing your tools efficiently. Tri-Tool Dashboard combines a Task Manager, Unit Converter, Notes, and Timer into one seamless interface to boost your productivity. It offers an all-in-one solution for managing tasks, converting units, taking notes, and tracking time, making it easier to stay organized and efficient throughout your day.
